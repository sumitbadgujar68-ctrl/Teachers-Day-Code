Why Teachers Matter

They debug minds

When curiosity throws an error, teachers step in with the cleanest pseudo-code: think, test, learn, repeat.

They bend reality

Every groundbreaking tech begins as a hypothesis a teacher took seriously. Permission to imagine — granted.

They light the path

From first principles to fierce pragmatism, they hand us the torch and say: your turn.

Our Note

To our teachers — you didn’t just prepare us for exams. You prepared us for unknowns. You made it safe to fail fast, to ask better questions, and to aim ridiculously high. If we ever build reactors, rockets, or bridges to other worlds, your fingerprints will be on the blueprints. Thank you for everything! — Sumit Badgujar & Kumar Kartikey
